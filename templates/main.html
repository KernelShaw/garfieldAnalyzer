What's the joke?










Inspired by Super Eyepatch Wolf's fantastic Garfield fever dream, this website aims to complete his woefully unpure spreadsheet on the nature of "jokes" in Garfield.

Garfield has, at the time of this writing, over 14,000 comic strips in existence. That is a lot of fuckin' Garfield.

I created this website partly out of necessity, and partly out of boredom. I "purposely" designed this website to be relatively lightweight, so you can help contribute whilst on your commute, during your lunch break, driving... maybe not that last one. Please don't check this website while driving.
