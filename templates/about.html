About The Project

What the hell is going on here?

I'm glad you asked! So, there's this dope lil' video made by a fella named Super Eyepatch Wolf. It's a look into the history of Garfield as a cultural icon, with an emphasis on his online presence. In the video, SEW decides to read like a thousand strips to determine what the most common Garfield joke is. Problem being, well, there's 16,000+ strips. This site aims to complete that data. Through the power of the internet, we can TRULY determine the most common Garfield joke!

How are you keeping track of all this?

You can find the spreadsheet here. Every time you submit a response, the relevant joke counter is incremented by one. Since a lot of these strips might have multiple responses for what the joke is, I've got two options: I either take the mode of an individual strip, or I let every response matter. Only time will tell which one I use.
